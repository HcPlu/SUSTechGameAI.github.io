The paper "Region-Focused Memetic Algorithms With Smart Initialization for Real-World Large-Scale Waste Collection Problems" by Wenxing Lan has been recognized with a main editor recommendation.s

2020-11-01

This recognition was featured in the October 2022 issue of the IEEE Computational Intelligence Society Newsletter. The paper presents novel memetic algorithms that are focused on specific regions and incorporate smart initialization techniques to efficiently solve large-scale waste collection problems in real-world scenarios. The elegance of the paper's methodology and its potential impact on the field make it a noteworthy contribution to the scientific community.
